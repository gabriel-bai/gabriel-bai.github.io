WHO AM I ?

Hello everybody ! I reside in Paris, and my name is Gabriel. I am 17 years old. My primary areas of interest encompass Computer Science and Geography. Furthermore, my most deeply held values revolve around the principles of sharing and mutual assistance. Alongside a group of friends, I have established an association named "Aotrom," signifying "Light" in the Scottish Gaelic language. Our mission involves extending aid to the underprivileged through the provision of essential kits. These kits encompass items such as coffee, hot meals, and hygiene products.
